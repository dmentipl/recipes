# Old fashioned

## Metadata

- **Labels**:: Cocktail, Drink

## Ingredients

- 60 ml whisky (bourbon or rye)
- 4 dashes aromatic bitters
- 1/2 tsp sugar syrup
- orange twist
- Maraschino cherry

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients.
2. Serve in a rocks glass.
3. Garnish with an orange twist and cherry.

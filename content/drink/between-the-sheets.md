## Ingredients

- 30 ml brandy
- 30 ml rum
- 30 ml triple sec
- 20 ml lemon juice
- orange twist

## Instructions

1. Shake ingredients.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with orange twist.

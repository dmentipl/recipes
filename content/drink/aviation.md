## Ingredients

- 50 ml Gin
- 20 ml Lemon juice
- 10 ml Maraschino liqueur
- 5 ml Sugar syrup
- 5ml crème de violette
- cherry

## Instructions

1. Shake ingredients (crème de violette optional).
2. Serve in cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with cherry.

## Ingredients

- 50 ml Rye whisky
- 25 ml Dry vermouth
- 10 ml Grenadine
- 4 dashes Orange bitters
- 15 ml Lemon juice
- lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Shake ingredients.
2. Serve in cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with lemon twist.

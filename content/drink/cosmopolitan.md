## Ingredients

- 60 ml Vodka (citron)
- 30 ml Triple sec
- 30 ml Lime juice
- 15 ml Cranberry juice
- lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Shake ingredients.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with lemon twist.

## Ingredients

- 30 ml Scotch
- 30 ml Oloroso Sherry
- 15 ml Lemon juice
- 15 ml Grenadine or redcurrant or raspberry syrup
- lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Shake ingredients.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with lemon twist.

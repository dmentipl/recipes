## Ingredients

- 60 ml Gin
- 30 ml Lime juice
- 15 ml Sugar syrup
- 1 Mint sprig
- 1 mint leaf

## Instructions

1. Shaken (without mint).
2. Rolled (with mint).
3. Serve in a cocktail glass.
4. Garnish with mint leaf.

## Ingredients

- 20 ml Scotch
- 20 ml Blood Orange juice
- 20 ml Sweet vermouth
- 20 ml Cherry Heering
- orange zest
- cherry

## Instructions

1. Shake ingredients.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
3. Garnish with a flamed orange zest and cherry.

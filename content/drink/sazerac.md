## Ingredients

- 60 ml Rye whisky
- 5 ml Sugar syrup
- 4 dashes Creole bitters
- 4 dashes Absinthe
- lemon twist

## Instructions

1. (Optional) rinse glass with absinthe.
2. Stirred.
3. Serve in a rocks glass without ice.
4. Garnish with a lemon twist.

## Ingredients

- 60 ml Rum
- 30 ml Grapefruit juice
- 15 ml Lime juice
- 15 ml Maraschino liqueur
- 5 ml Sugar syrup
- lime slice

## Instructions

1. Shake with ice.
2. Serve in a cocktail glass
3. Garnish with lime slice.

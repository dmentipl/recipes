## Ingredients

- 60 ml Bourbon
- 30 ml Lemon juice
- 15 ml Sugar syrup
- 4 dashes Orange Bitters
- 1/2 Egg white (optional)
- cherry
- orange twist

## Instructions

1. Dry shake ingredients.
2. Then shake with ice and double strain.
3. Serve in a rocks glass over fresh ice.
4. Garnish with orange twist and cherry.

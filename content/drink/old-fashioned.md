## Ingredients

- 60 ml Rye whisky or Bourbon
- 5 ml Sugar syrup
- 4 dashes Aromatic bitters
- orange or lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Stir ingredients.
2. Serve in a rocks glass over ice.
3. Garnish with a twist.

## Ingredients

- 45 ml Vodka
- 90 ml Tomato juice
- 15 ml Lemon juice
- 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
- 2 dashes Hot sauce
- pinch celery salt
- pinch pepper
- celery stalk
- lemon wedge

## Instructions

1. Build in highball glass over ice.
2. Garnish with celery and lemon.

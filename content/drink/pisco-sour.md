## Ingredients

- 45 ml Pisco
- 30 ml Lime juice
- 15 ml Sugar syrup
- 1/2 Egg white
- Aromatic bitters
- lime slice

## Instructions

1. Dry shake ingredients.
2. Then shake with ice.
3. Serve in a rocks glass without ice.
4. Garnish with drops of bitters and a lime slice.

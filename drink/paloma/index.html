Paloma¶

Metadata¶

  • Labels:: Cocktail, Drink

Ingredients #1¶

  • 60 ml tequila

  • 120 ml grapefruit soda

  • 1 tsp lime

  • pinch of salt

Instructions #1¶

  1. Mix together in a highball glass over ice.

  2. Garnish with lime wedge.

Ingredients #2¶

  • 45 ml tequila

  • 30 ml grapefruit juice

  • 15 ml lime juice

  • 1 tsp agave syrup

  • splash of soda water (optional)

  • lime or grapefruit wedge

Instructions #2¶

  1. Shake ingredients.

  2. Serve in a Collins glass with ice.

  3. Garnish with lime or grapefruit wedge.

Whiskey sour¶

Metadata¶

  • Labels:: Cocktail, Drink

Ingredients¶

  • 60 ml bourbon whiskey

  • 30 ml lemon juice

  • 1 tsp syrup

  • 1/2 egg white (optional)

  • maraschino cherry

  • orange slice

Instructions¶

  1. Dry shake bourbon, lemon, syrup, and egg white.

  2. Then shake with ice.

  3. Serve in a rocks glass.

  4. Garnish with orange slice and cherry.

Mojito¶

Metadata¶

  • Labels:: Cocktail, Drink

Ingredients¶

  • 45 ml rum

  • 30 ml lime juice

  • 15 ml sugar syrup

  • a few mint leaves

  • 45 ml soda water

  • mint sprig

Instructions¶

  1. Build in highball glass.

  2. Muddle mint with sugar and lime juice

  3. Add rum, ice, and soda water.

  4. Garnish with a mint sprig.

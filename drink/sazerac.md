# Sazerac

## Metadata

- **Labels**:: Cocktail, Drink

## Ingredients

- 60 ml Rye whisky or Cognac (brandy)
- 1/2 tsp absinthe or Pastis
- 1/2 tsp sugar syrup
- 4 dashes Peychaud's bitters
- lemon twist

## Instructions

1. Stirred.
2. (Optional) absinthe rinse glass.
3. Serve in a rocks glass.
4. Garnish with a lemon twist.

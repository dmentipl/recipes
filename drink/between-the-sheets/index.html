Between the sheets

#brandy #lemon-juice #rum #triple-sec

Ingredients

  • 30 ml Brandy
  • 30 ml Rum
  • 30 ml Triple sec
  • 20 ml Lemon juice
  • orange twist

Instructions

  1. Shake ingredients.
  2. Serve in a cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with orange twist.

Negroni¶

Metadata¶

  • Labels:: Cocktail, Drink

Ingredients¶

  • 30 ml gin

  • 30 ml Campari

  • 45 ml sweet vermouth

  • 1/2 slice orange

Instructions¶

  1. Stir ingredients.

  2. Serve in rocks glass over ice.

  3. Garnish with orange slice.

Tom Collins¶

Metadata¶

  • Labels:: Cocktail, Drink

Ingredients¶

  • 45 ml gin

  • 30 ml lemon juice

  • 15 ml sugar syrup

  • 60 ml soda water

  • lemon slice

Instructions¶

  1. Build in highball glass over ice.

  2. Garnish with lemon slice.

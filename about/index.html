About

Hi, I’m Daniel Mentiplay. I love eating and cooking, and drinking and mixing drinks. I’m lucky to live in Melbourne, Australia where we have access to high quality ingredients and great restaurants and bars.

This is where I keep collections of recipes for eating and drinking to share with family, friends, and others. Feel free to explore. Some recipes I’ve reworked from recipes found online or in books, with attribution. Other recipes I’ve created myself.

Notes

Note that for cocktail recipes I prefer a slightly drier drink than seems to be common (adjust according to your taste), and I use rich sugar syrup which is 2:1 sugar-to-water by volume, as opposed to simple syrup which is 1:1.
